This is a rotational program where participants are given the opportunity to build a strong foundation in leadership skills, financial services acumen, strategy, innovation and creativity, storytelling, and project management.

What do i do?

Within the LDP Program, I am on the Technology and Innovation - Experience Design Track. The Design team is responsible for taking complex problems and transforming them into world class client experiences for Truist’s digital products. In this program we are responsible for working on innovative projects and assignments within the Design team and will gain experience within distinct disciplines of Experience Design (UX/UI), User Experience Research, Service Design and Inclusive Design (ADA/Accessibility).

• Translate user needs and business requirements into compelling experiences

• Create user flows, wireframes and interactive prototypes for a variety of devices and interfaces

• Examine user interfaces/experiences in relation to current user stories and roadmap to suggest enhancements for the future

• Learn and incorporate Truist branding standards, guidelines, and digital design system

• Collaborate with a cross-functional team on innovative projects

• Review and provide feedback on time estimates, project requirements and specifications

• Work closely with the Digital Product and Enterprise Technology development teams to ensure features are implemented as intended and meet design quality, accessibility and usability expectations
